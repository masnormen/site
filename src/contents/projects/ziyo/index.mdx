In the beginning of 2024, I built [Ziyo](https://ziyo.nourman.com/), a stupidly simple online Kanji dictionary and search engine. It is heavily inspired by [Jisho](http://jisho.org/) and an iOS application named *Shirabe Jisho*. With Ziyo, you can search across thousands of kanji and view its readings, meanings, variations, and example sentences. This makes Ziyo very useful for Japanese learner who wants to do a quick search about a kanji.

# Features

Ziyo can search a kanji by:

- Onyomi reading (Kana/Latin)
- Kunyomi reading (Kana/Latin)
- Chinese Mandarin reading (Pinyin readings)
- Korean reading (Hangul/Latin - Hanja readings)
- Meanings

- Nx for monorepo management

- Tailwind + Shadcn UI
- Hono.js for backend
